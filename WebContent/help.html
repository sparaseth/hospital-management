About Patient Care @ Hartford Hospital

Everyone at Hartford Hospital considers our patients and their families to be partners in health care.

Hartford Hospital's Health Care Team is dedicated to anticipating and exceeding patients' needs. The information in this website will help patients with concerns and questions about Hartford Hospital and their stay, as well as planning resources for after-hospital recuperation.

As a patient, we want your stay at Hartford Hospital to be as pleasant and comfortable as possible.

About us

Hartford Hospital is an acute care hospital, located in the south end of Hartford Connecticut. The Hospital came into being in 1854 when a catastrophic explosion created the need to treat the many injured. In 1994, The Institute of Living, a psychiatric hospital founded in 1822, became the Mental Health Division of Hartford Hospital.

Today, Hartford Hospital is a major tertiary care and community health care center, serving a statewide patient population.

The hospital has been a recipient of numerous highly prestigious national awards, recognizing the institution for its medical excellence, as well as its work in the areas of community relations, minority recruitment and the provision of health services within a diverse population.
